Study on Challenges faced by Young Social Entrepreneurs in Vietnam

Chào mn/anh/chị, em đang tìm hiểu về các khó khăn của người trẻ làm Social Entrepreneur ở Việt Nam để xây dựng giải pháp cho Việt Nam. Em xin gửi form để anh/chị có thể giúp điền báo cáo gọn. Kết quả nghiên cứu (và 1 món quà nhỏ) sẽ được chia sẻ với người tham gia và cộng đồng sau khi buổi họp kết thúc. Cảm ơn mn/anh/chị.

[English]

Dear Young Social Entrepreneurs,

Thank you for participating in this study. I am a working professional trying to learn more about challenges faced by social entrepreneurs, the purpose of this form is to understand the challenges you face as young social entrepreneurs in Vietnam. Your responses will help me identify areas where support is needed most, so I can help provide solutions to enhance your entrepreneurship journey and create meaningful outcomes.

I value your insights and experiences. The study results will be shared with all participants once the study is completeda, along with a small gift from me. Your responses will remain confidential and will only be used for research purposes.

Thank you for your time and contribution to this important study!
